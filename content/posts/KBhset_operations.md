## multiplication {#multiplication}

For set \\(\mathcal{P}\\), matrix \\(A\\):

\begin{equation}
A \mathcal{P} = \qty {Ap \mid p \in \mathcal{P}}
\end{equation}


## adding {#adding}

For sets \\(\mathcal{P}, \mathcal{Q}\\):

\begin{equation}
\mathcal{P} \oplus \mathcal{Q} = \qty {p+q \mid p \in \mathcal{P}, q \in \mathcal{Q}}
\end{equation}
