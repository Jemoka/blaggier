\begin{equation}
softmax\qty(x\_{i})  = \frac{\exp(x\_{i})}{\sum\_{j=1}^{n} \exp(x\_{j})}
\end{equation}

its "softmax"!

-   **max**: amplifies the probability of the largest \\(x\_{i}\\)
-   **soft**: because it assign some probability to smaller \\(x\_{j}\\) as well
