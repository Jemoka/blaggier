[protected group]({{< relref "KBhprotected_group.md" >}})s are features that one shouldn't use: as in, these cannot be used:

-   race
-   color
-   national origin
-   religion
-   age
-   sex and gender
-   sexual orientation
-   physical or mental disability
-   reprisal (grudges)
