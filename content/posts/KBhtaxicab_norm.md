The [taxicab norm]({{< relref "KBhtaxicab_norm.md" >}}) is a norm against a gridded system; it should follow the same properties of the [norm]({{< relref "KBhnorm.md" >}}), but not [inner product]({{< relref "KBhinner_product.md" >}})s.
