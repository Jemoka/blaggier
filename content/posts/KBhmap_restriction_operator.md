Suppose \\(T \in \mathcal{L}(V)\\), and \\(U \subset V\\), an [invariant subspace]({{< relref "KBhinvariant_subspace.md" >}}) under \\(T\\). Then:

\begin{equation}
T|\_{U}(u) = Tu,\ \forall u \in U
\end{equation}

where \\(T|\_{U} \in \mathcal{L}(U)\\)
