...is a series for which pairs cancel out:

\begin{equation}
(1-x)(1+x+ \dots  + x^{n-1})
\end{equation}

you will note that, though the expansion of this result, pairs of:

\begin{equation}
-x^{j} + x^{j}
\end{equation}

form. And you will note those cancel. Hence this is a telescoping series.
