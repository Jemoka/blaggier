If an outcome can be from sets \\(A=m\\) **or** \\(B=n\\) with no overlaps, where \\(A \cap B = \emptyset\\), then, the total number of outcomes are \\(|A| + |B| = m+n\\)

If there are overlap:

\begin{equation}
N = |A|+|B| - |A \cap B|
\end{equation}
