DeMorgan's Law

Last edited: August 8, 2025

Suppose you have two non mutually exclusive sets \(E\) or \(F\).

DeMorgan’s Law:

\begin{equation} (E\ and\ F)^{C} = (E^{C}\ or\ F^{C}) \end{equation}

\begin{equation} (E\ or\ F)^{C} = (E^{C}\ and\ F^{C}) \end{equation}
