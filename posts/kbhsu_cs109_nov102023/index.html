Interesting Factoids

\(\mathbb{E}[Y]\) =

  • \(x=1\): 3
  • \(x=2\): 5 + Y
  • \(x=3\): 7 + Y

\begin{equation} \mathbb{E}[Y] = 3 \cdot \frac{1}{3} + (5+ \mathbb{E}[Y]) \cdot \frac{1}{3} + (7+ \mathbb{E}[Y]) \cdot \frac{1}{3} \end{equation}
